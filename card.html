Sea Turtle

Sea turtles have been around for over 100 million years!

  • Not land dwellers: Have fins and spend most of their time swimming, returning only to lay eggs
  • Known for longevity: Live around 80 years
  • Endangered species: Classified as endangered according to the Endangered Species Act
  • Predators: Feast includes jellyfish, crabs, and mollusks to name a few

Sea turtles are green, black, or yellow creatures versions of the classic, shelled creature we've come to know and love. They inhabit nearly every temperate body of water in the world.
